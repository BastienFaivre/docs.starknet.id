#### What is starknet.id ?

Starknet.id is an identity and naming protocol built on top of Starknet that will permit you to register native .stark domains to Starknet (like .eth domains on ethereum). Starknet id have a different team than the Starknet/Starkware team and we're not affiliated with them.

**What is the difference between an identity and a domain ?** An identity is an NFT that everyone can mint for free. It represents your starknet identity and you'll be able to connect your social media to it. You can also register a .stark domain on top of it if you want (and you'll have to pay for it). If you do then you'll have an identity linked with a domain.

Differences between Identity and Identity linked with a domain

[On Mintsquare](https://mintsquare.io/collection/starknet/starknet-id/activities), you are only able to trade starknet identities linked with a domain. An identity not linked with a domain can be useful because it'll represent your identity on Starknet however they do not have the same value as an identity linked with a domain, it's the reason why you should not trade them.
